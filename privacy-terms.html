Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Zack Simon ("I", "me") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when signing up for The Build Log newsletter.

Information Collected

When you subscribe, I collect:

  • Email address (required)
  • Phone number (optional, for SMS updates)

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used solely to send you The Build Log — a periodic newsletter covering what I'm building, decisions I'm making, and progress updates. Specifically:

  • Your email address is used to send newsletter emails.
  • Your phone number, if provided, is used to send SMS text message updates.

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Subscriber information is stored securely on AWS (Amazon Web Services) infrastructure located in the United States.

Opting Out

  • Email: Reply to any email with "unsubscribe" or use the unsubscribe link included in every message.
  • SMS: Reply STOP to any text message to unsubscribe immediately. Reply HELP for help.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact me at zacksimon13@gmail.com.

By subscribing to The Build Log, you agree to the following terms.

  • Message frequency varies. You may receive up to a few messages per week.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • To stop receiving SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

  • Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of any purchase or service.

Email Messaging Terms

  • By providing your email address, you consent to receive periodic emails from Zack Simon related to The Build Log.
